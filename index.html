Stay Home. Stay Safe.

According to the National Center for Injury Prevention and Control, falls are the leading cause of all unintentional, nonfatal injuries for people of all ages. We have found that the bathroom becomes a frequent site of these falls where water and slick surfaces are present.

Experts offer many tips on how to prevent these kinds of accidents. They all agree on one point: Correctly installing sturdy safety bars in critical areas around your home is at the top of everyone's safety checklist.

Specializing in Safety Bars

Paul's Grab Bars specializes in expertly installing safety grab bars in homes, condominiums, and apartments across the greater Twin Cities area. With over 70,000 bars installed, owner Paul Althoff is your best man for the job. With more than 500 grab bars in stock—and more than 150 grab bars of various styles and lengths on Paul's truck when we come to your home—we are ready to install that day.
