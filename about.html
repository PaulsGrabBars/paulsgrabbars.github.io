Since he opened his business in 1983, Paul Althoff (shown installing a grab bar above) has installed over 34,000 grab bars in homes. When it comes to your safety and that of your family, doesn't it make sense to hire an expert?

Owner Paul Althoff says:

My grandfather Frank (shown at left), who lived to be 97 years old, was very important to me. Having a special grandfather like Frank helped me to love working with older people.

Born in 1877, there were a lot of changes he had to make in his 97 years. I believe change can be good. Putting in grab bars will be a good change for you or your loved ones.

We use a Minnesota-based manufacturer to supply us with stainless steel grab bars that are very high quality and come in a variety of colors and sizes. Working with a local manufacturer allows us to do custom grab bars that are needed in some situations because of the stud location, or the demands of individual lifestyles.
