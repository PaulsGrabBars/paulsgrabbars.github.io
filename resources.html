Resources & Links (listed alphabetically)

At Paul's Grab Bars we install the right grab bars and poles in the right places to make their homes safe for our clients. But sometimes clients need additional services and help of a different kind. We have worked with the people and the firms listed here, and we personally recommend them to our clients.— Paul and Barb
Above & Beyond Senior Services, call for a free consultation (612) 965-5122 www.aboveservices.com

A guidance and support service for seniors who want a way of life that is less demanding but allows for prolonging personal freedom. They provide both "age in place" and move management services from a therapist's perspective. Talk to Allison Bakke, OTR/L, and mention our name.

AccessAbility Options, Inc., call for free estimate and free site visit: (763) 5791-6789 www.AccessOptionsMn.com

If you need to remodel, this is a full-service design/build firm with years of experience that specialize in developing options that offer a functional, safe, attractive, yet economical solution to meet the special accessibility needs of their clients. Talk to Randy or Beth Behling when you call.

Active Medical, 4216 Minnetonka Blvd., St. Louis Park, MN 55416 952-922-2420  www.active-medical.com

This medical retail store offers, in addition to a huge supply of equipment and a willingness to custom order items, delivery and repair on all of their items. We are always confident that our clients will receive both outstanding service and a huge selection of products designed to make life safer and more accessible, at home and away. Ask for Jane Pate and mention our name.

Anchor Iron Company, 12358 Boone Ave., Savage, MN 55378 (952) 445-3030 www.anchoriron.com

This family-owned business has helped our clients with the custom purchase and installation of both interior and exterior railings for their home, walkways, driveways and windows. All items are custom-made.
